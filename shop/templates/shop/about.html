About Us

Welcome to our vineyard, where the essence of tradition intertwines with innovative, sustainable practices to create wines that are both exquisite and earth-friendly. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our meticulous approach to biological agriculture, respect for natural cycles, and dedication to fair and ethical production.

Embracing Biological Agriculture

Our vineyard thrives on the principles of biological agriculture, ensuring that every aspect of our cultivation is sustainable and environmentally friendly. Here's how we achieve this:

  • Natural Soil Enrichment: We enhance soil health through composting organic waste and employing green manures, which replenish essential nutrients and improve soil structure. This natural enrichment fosters healthy, resilient vines.
  • Biodiversity and Ecosystem Balance: We maintain a balanced ecosystem by encouraging biodiversity. Beneficial insects and natural predators play a crucial role in pest control, reducing the need for synthetic pesticides. This harmony ensures healthier vines and a more robust vineyard environment.
  • Organic Farming Practices: Our commitment to organic farming means avoiding synthetic chemicals and fertilizers. Instead, we use organic composts, cover crops, and natural minerals to nourish our vines, promoting long-term soil fertility and ecological balance.

The Influence of Astronomical Phases

Our agricultural practices are deeply rooted in the rhythms of nature. We align our planting and harvesting schedules with the lunar cycles and planetary positions, a tradition that has been passed down through generations. This biodynamic approach includes:

  • Lunar Calendar: By following the lunar calendar, we time our activities to enhance the vitality and growth of our vines. For instance, we plant during the waxing moon to encourage strong, upward growth and harvest during the waning moon for optimal fruit quality.
  • Planetary Influence: We also consider the positions of planets, as they are believed to influence plant health and soil vitality. This cosmic timing ensures that our grapes develop with optimal energy and resilience, resulting in superior wines.

Commitment to Fair Prices

Fairness and ethics are foundational to our vineyard's operations. We ensure fair prices for our winemakers and workers by:

  • Transparent Pricing: We maintain transparent pricing practices, allowing consumers to understand the true value of our wines. This transparency builds trust and fosters a strong connection between our vineyard and our customers.
  • Equitable Compensation: We provide just and fair wages to everyone involved in our winemaking process. This ethical stance supports our local community and ensures that our workforce is motivated and valued.
  • Sustainable Economics: By fostering fair trade practices, we contribute to a sustainable economic model that benefits both producers and consumers. This approach guarantees that our high-quality wines are produced in an ethically responsible manner.

Short Production Pathways

Our dedication to short production pathways enhances both the quality of our wines and our commitment to sustainability. This approach includes:

  • Local Sourcing: We source our grapes and materials locally, minimizing transportation time and preserving the freshness and integrity of our grapes.
  • Efficient Processing: Our streamlined production processes ensure that grapes move quickly from vine to bottle, maintaining their natural flavors and reducing the need for preservatives.
  • Environmental Impact: By shortening our supply chain, we significantly reduce our carbon footprint. This commitment to eco-friendly practices ensures that our vineyard remains sustainable and responsible.

A Tradition of Excellence

Our vineyard stands as a testament to the art of winemaking, honed over generations. Each bottle we produce is a labor of love, reflecting our deep respect for the land and our dedication to quality. We invite you to explore our wines, where tradition meets innovation and every sip tells a story of our passionate pursuit of perfection.


Join us at our vineyard, where the beauty of nature and the excellence of our craft come together to create truly remarkable wines. Here, every bottle is a celebration of our heritage, our commitment to sustainability, and our unwavering passion for winemaking.
